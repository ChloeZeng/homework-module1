Explore the Great Cliffs

The Great Cliffs of California are famous for their dramatic ocean views, unique rock formations, and rich history. Visitors come from all over the world to hike, climb, and admire these natural wonders.

Highlights of Great Cliffs

  • Unique rock formations shaped by centuries of erosion
  • Popular hiking trails with breathtaking views
  • Perfect spots for sunrise and sunset photography
  • Wildlife habitats including seabirds and dolphins

Cliffs Information
